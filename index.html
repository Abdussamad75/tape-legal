Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2025

Welcome to Tape ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Tape mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, account type (individual/business), business address (for business accounts)
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, website, social media handles (Facebook, Instagram), location (country and state/region)
  • Client Data: Client names, measurements, garment preferences, notes
  • Order Information: Order details, phone numbers, amounts, due dates, payment information, order status
  • Notes: Personal notes and reminders you create within the app

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Network Information: Internet connectivity status (to ensure app functionality)

2. Permissions We Request

2.1 Camera Access

Why: To allow you to take photos for profile pictures and to scan QR codes for sharing client measurements.

When: Only when you explicitly choose to take a photo or scan a QR code.

Data Storage: Photos are stored securely in our cloud storage. QR code scans are processed immediately and not stored.

2.2 Photo Library / Storage Access

Why: To allow you to upload existing photos for your profile picture.

When: Only when you explicitly choose to select a photo from your gallery.

Data Storage: Selected photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage.

2.4 Notifications (Optional)

Why: To send you reminders about order due dates and important app updates.

When: Only if you grant notification permission.

Control: You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Enable you to store client measurements, manage orders, and organize your tailoring business
  • Connect Users: Help you find and connect with other tailors and businesses based on your state and country
  • Improve App: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Security: Protect your account from unauthorized access and detect fraudulent activity
  • Communication: Send important updates about your account, orders, and app changes

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Information We Share

With Other Users (Controlled by You):

  • Your profile information (name, country, state, account type) is visible to other users in the "Find People" section
  • Client measurements can only be shared via QR code that YOU generate and share

With Service Providers:

  • Appwrite (cloud hosting) - stores your data securely in Frankfurt, Germany
  • Cloud storage providers - store profile photos and images

4.2 Information We Never Share

  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do NOT share your client data with advertisers
  • We do NOT use your measurements or order data for marketing purposes

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers operated by Appwrite, located in Frankfurt, Germany. All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

5.2 Security Measures

  • Password encryption using secure hashing algorithms
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and permission systems

5.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is stored as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all your personal data, clients, measurements, orders, and notes are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes, then permanently deleted

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your information within the app
  • Edit Your Data: Update your profile, clients, measurements, and orders at any time
  • Download Your Data: Export your measurements and client data via QR codes
  • Delete Your Data: Delete individual clients, measurements, orders, or your entire account

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • Choose your country and state
  • Control who can connect with you
  • Disconnect from users at any time
  • Control notification preferences

6.3 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. This will:

  • Permanently delete all your personal information
  • Delete all client records and measurements
  • Delete all orders and analytics data
  • Delete all notes and connections
  • Remove your profile from "Find People"

This action cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

Tape is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under the applicable age, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your country of residence, including Germany (where our servers are located). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: privacy@tape.app

In-App: Use the Help screen to submit inquiries
